Submitting results

Strava Users

The easiest way to access this site is with a Strava account which is a member of the Romsey Road Runners Strava Club. These accounts will automatically capture race results from Strava, and will be immediately activated.

First make sure you're a member of the Strava Club and then click "Connect with Strava" on the Sign In page. This will allow us to automatically capture your times from Strava if the activity has a privacy setting of "Followers Only" or "Everyone".

Make sure your race attempts include "Club Challenge Road" in the title for road races or "Club Challenge Trail" for trail.

We only capture activities which are within a reasonable margin of error (-50m +100m) of the actual course distance (the race pages). If your activity is too long here's how to crop activities. If it is too short due to GPS problems, please contact us at results@romseyroadrunners.co.uk.

Usually activities will be captured from Strava immediately but this can take up to two hours. If it meets the requirements above but isn't showing up, try slightly editing the title on Strava which will trigger an update.

Non-Strava Users

Please register using the "Manual Registration" button on the Sign In page and enter all your details. You will need to wait for us to check you are a club member and activate your account.

You will then be able to use the "Submit Result" button above to manually upload evidence of your results. Ideally this should be a GPX file which you can export from most watches/services. However we will accept a photo of your watch (or equivalent) showing time and distance.

If you are not comfortable signing up for this site, you can email results@romseyroadrunners.co.uk with your time and evidence and we will upload it for you.

Any problems?

Please email rrrweb@romseyroadrunners.co.uk for help.
